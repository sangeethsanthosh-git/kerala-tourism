Destinations

Explore more of Kerala

Kerala offers hill stations, backwaters, and beaches for every kind of traveler. Here are a few beautiful places to start with.

Munnar

Known for tea gardens, winding roads, and fresh mountain air, Munnar is perfect for a cool and scenic retreat.

Alleppey

Famous for houseboats and peaceful canals, Alleppey gives travelers a slow and refreshing backwater experience.

Kovalam

With golden sand, coastal views, and a laid-back atmosphere, Kovalam is one of Kerala's best beach destinations.

More places to add to your list

Thekkady, Wayanad, Vagamon, Varkala, and Bekal are also popular choices for wildlife, hills, and coastal views.
