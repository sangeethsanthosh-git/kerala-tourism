About Kerala Tourism

Why Kerala is a favorite travel destination

Kerala is known for its green landscapes, peaceful waterways, traditional culture, and welcoming hospitality. It is a great place for family vacations, honeymoon trips, nature stays, and short relaxing holidays.

Travelers come here to enjoy cool hill stations, gentle houseboat rides, fresh local food, cultural performances, and sunset views by the sea. Every part of Kerala offers a different and refreshing experience.

Travel Highlights

  • Hill stations with tea gardens and misty views
  • Backwater cruises through quiet village canals
  • Beautiful beaches with calm evening sunsets
  • Rich culture, local cuisine, and warm hospitality

Featured Destinations

Top places to explore in Kerala

Start your journey with these three popular destinations, each known for a unique side of Kerala tourism.

Munnar

Munnar is a peaceful hill station famous for its tea plantations, cool weather, and beautiful mountain views.

Alleppey

Alleppey is well known for its backwaters, houseboat stays, coconut-lined canals, and relaxing village scenery.

Kovalam

Kovalam is a popular beach destination with soft sands, ocean views, fresh seafood, and calm coastal charm.
